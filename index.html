A Bit About Me

When I was a kid my dad pushed for my brother and I to learn computers. I took to it like a fish to water. From 8 onwards my summers were spent indoors working away on simple scripting languages and later on some game modifications.

I won't lie, it wasn't easy getting past my "it needs to be perfect all the time" streak. In fact I still have that streak, I've just learned to fix and perfect as you go instead of making it perfect on the first go-round.

I absolutely love a challenge, critisism of my work used to cause me to clam up a bit. Over time I learned to take the constructive side of critisism and use it to better myself and the content I produce.

None of this would be possible without my amazing wife who puts up with my nose being buried in a book or in code for hours at a time every day. I want to provide the best life I can for her, and I'm good at tech and I love tech, this is how I plan to provide for her the rest of our lives.
